# Business Requirements

## Project Name
Real Estate Business Intelligence Platform

## Business Problem

Commercial real estate organizations manage thousands of buildings across multiple cities and states. Large volumes of operational, maintenance, occupancy, and energy data are generated every month.

Due to inconsistent data formats, manual reporting processes, and limited visibility into key performance indicators, management faces challenges in identifying cost optimization opportunities and making data-driven decisions.

This platform provides a centralized Business Intelligence solution that automates data integration, validation, analysis, benchmarking, and reporting.

---

## Objectives

- Centralize building-related operational data
- Improve data quality through validation
- Monitor operational KPIs
- Benchmark building performance
- Analyze energy consumption
- Reduce manual reporting
- Enable executive decision-making
- Automate monthly reports

---

## Users

- Executive Management
- Facility Managers
- Business Analysts
- Property Managers
- Operations Team
- Sustainability Team

---

## Business Questions

1. Which buildings have the highest operating costs?
2. Which cities have the highest maintenance expenses?
3. Which buildings exceed benchmark costs?
4. Which customers consume the most energy?
5. Which buildings have poor energy efficiency?
6. What is the monthly operating cost trend?
7. Which maintenance activities are most expensive?
8. What is the occupancy rate across the portfolio?
9. Which buildings generate the highest CO₂ emissions?
10. Which datasets contain data quality issues?

---

## Success Criteria

- Automated reporting
- Accurate KPI calculation
- Reliable benchmarking
- Interactive dashboards
- Reduced manual effort
- Data-driven insights
