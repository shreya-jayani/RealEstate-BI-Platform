# Business Entities

## Building

Represents every commercial building managed by a customer.

---

## Customer

Organization that owns one or more buildings.

---

## Location

City and State where a building is located.

---

## Building Type

Office, Hospital, Warehouse, Retail, etc.

---

## Operating Cost

Monthly expenses related to building operations.

---

## Energy Consumption

Monthly electricity, gas and water usage.

---

## Maintenance

Repairs and preventive maintenance.

---

## Inspection

Safety and compliance inspections.

---

## Benchmark

Comparison against similar buildings.

---

## Occupancy

Monthly occupancy information.
